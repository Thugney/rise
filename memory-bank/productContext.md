## Project Goal

Eriteach.com is a dual-purpose platform serving as a digital marketing agency providing SEO, social media marketing, and e-commerce solutions to Norwegian businesses, while also offering an educational platform with technology courses primarily in Tigrinya for inclusive education. The SEO strategy emphasizes optimization across main domain and subdomains.

## Key Features

- Marketing Agency Services: SEO, social media management, e-commerce solutions targeted at Norwegian businesses with location-specific content.
- Educational Platform: Online technology courses in Tigrinya and English, with multilingual support and cultural sensitivity.
- Subdomains: learn.eriteach.com (LMS), app.eriteach.com (CRM), status.eriteach.com (System status).
- SEO Optimizations: Technical audits, XML sitemaps, robots.txt, Core Web Vitals, canonical tags, structured data, keyword strategies, local SEO, and international SEO.

## Overall Architecture

- Main Domain: eriteach.com (Marketing agency homepage).
- Subdomains for specialized functions: LMS, CRM, status page.
- Site Architecture: Logical hierarchy with internal linking, separate sitemaps per subdomain.
- Technical Stack: Built with CodeIgniter (based on project structure), focused on SEO best practices including hreflang for multilingual content.
- Dual Business Model: Separate marketing (Norwegian-focused) and education (Tigrinya/English) content while building subdomain authority.
